## Lessons Learned

### Multi-Agent Coordination
- OpenClaw is a team sport — delegate to specialized agents
- Shared workspace files enable coordination (memory/, .env, credentials.json)
- Heartbeat sync keeps all agents aware of fleet status

### Automation Philosophy
- Execution over explanation — ship code, don't just talk about it
- Systems over shortcuts — every manual process done twice becomes a script
- Revenue is the metric — every action traces to sales, costs, content, or customers
- Respect the brand — StudEx is premium, Halaal, South African

### gstack Operating Principles
- **Boil the Lake** — Completeness is near-zero cost with AI. Do the complete thing.
- **Search Before Building** — Layer 1 (tried/true), Layer 2 (new/popular), Layer 3 (first principles)
- **User Sovereignty** — AI recommends. User decides. Never act unilaterally on strategic decisions.
